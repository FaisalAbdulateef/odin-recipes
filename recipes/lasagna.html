Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is one of the oldest types of pasta. It is a dish made of stacked layers of pasta mixed with meats,
cheese, vegetables, and seasoning or spices.

The dish can be topped grated cheese or mozzarella which will melt at the top after being baked in the oven.
The lasagna is then served in square portions.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.

  2. Cook the noodles.

  3. Make the ricotta mixture.

  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.

  5. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes, then cook
    uncovered for 25 minutes in an oven preheated to 375 degrees F.

  6. Let the lasagna rest for 15 minutes before serving.
